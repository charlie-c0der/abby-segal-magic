About the Performer

Crafting the Impossible

I'm Abby Segal, a Chicago-based magician who specializes in close-up magic and sleight of hand. My approach combines technical precision with psychological insight to create moments that feel both mysterious and intimate.

After years of studying the craft, I've developed a style that focuses on the human connection at the heart of every magical moment. Whether performing at the Chicago Magic Lounge or at private events across the city, my goal is always the same: to create experiences that live long after the cards are put away.

Based in Chicago, available for private events, corporate functions, and special occasions throughout the greater Chicagoland area.
